## Philosophy

Plato. *The Republic*. Translated by G.M.A. Grube. Indianapolis: Hackett Publishing, 1992.

Plato. *Phaedrus*. Translated by Alexander Nehamas and Paul Woodruff. Indianapolis: Hackett Publishing, 1995.

Aristotle. *Metaphysics*. Translated by Hugh Lawson-Tancred. London: Penguin Classics, 1998.

Plotinus. *The Enneads*. Translated by Stephen MacKenna. London: Penguin Classics, 1991.

Ockham, William of. *Philosophical Writings: A Selection*. Translated by Philotheus Boehner. Indianapolis: Hackett Publishing, 1990.

Heidegger, Martin. *Being and Time*. Translated by John Macquarrie and Edward Robinson. New York: Harper & Row, 1962.

Heidegger, Martin. "The Question Concerning Technology." In *The Question Concerning Technology and Other Essays*, translated by William Lovitt, 3–35. New York: Harper & Row, 1977.

Heidegger, Martin. "Building Dwelling Thinking." In *Poetry, Language, Thought*, translated by Albert Hofstadter, 141–160. New York: Harper & Row, 1971.

Nietzsche, Friedrich. *Twilight of the Idols*. Translated by Duncan Large. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1998.

Deleuze, Gilles. "Plato and the Simulacrum." Translated by Rosalind Krauss. *October* 27 (1983): 45–56.

## Psychology and Consciousness

Jaynes, Julian. *The Origin of Consciousness in the Breakdown of the Bicameral Mind*. Boston: Houghton Mifflin, 1976.

Sapolsky, Robert M. *Determined: A Science of Life Without Free Will*. New York: Penguin Press, 2023.

Sapolsky, Robert M. *Behave: The Biology of Humans at Our Best and Worst*. New York: Penguin Press, 2017.

Ligotti, Thomas. *The Conspiracy Against the Human Race: A Contrivance of Horror*. New York: Hippocampus Press, 2010.

## Sociology and Civilization

Durkheim, Émile. *The Division of Labour in Society*. Translated by W.D. Halls. New York: Free Press, 1984.

Durkheim, Émile. *The Elementary Forms of Religious Life*. Translated by Karen E. Fields. New York: Free Press, 1995.

Eliade, Mircea. *The Sacred and the Profane: The Nature of Religion*. Translated by Willard R. Trask. New York: Harcourt, 1959.

Eliade, Mircea. *The Myth of the Eternal Return: Cosmos and History*. Translated by Willard R. Trask. Princeton: Princeton University Press, 1954.

## Architecture and Space

Augé, Marc. *Non-Places: Introduction to an Anthropology of Supermodernity*. Translated by John Howe. London: Verso, 1995.

Bachelard, Gaston. *The Poetics of Space*. Translated by Maria Jolas. Boston: Beacon Press, 1994.
